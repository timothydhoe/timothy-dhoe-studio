Wegkapitein

the brief

Wegkapitein bicycles initiates the feeling cyclists know all to well: suffering, not giving in and working hard to reach the finish line. The brand differentiates itself by not talking about the technical aspects of its frames but initiates the emotional connection people have with the sport.

the opportunity

Cycle sports have been massively popular in Belgium since the 1920s and reaching its peak in the 1950s and 60s. Local heroes who performed really well in races were idolised to the level that the phrase "Cycling is religion" will sound very familiar to Belgian readers.

Wegkapitein is an inclusive, progressive cycling brand designed for the connoiseur, and its identity needed to represent that in an appealing and approachable way without trying too hard. To do so, we took inspiration from modernistic designs and reshaped them to appeal more to the contemporary audience.
