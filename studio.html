Timothy D'hoe studio is a creative branding and design studio. We create purpose, ideas and stories to develop unique brand experiences.

about the studio

We create brands, choices, awareness, understanding and conversation. We help brands change things and evolve for the better. All by design.

The studio is run by designer Timothy D'hoe. As a creative partner, it's our job to go beyond the brief. You'll be collaborating with a guy who's thoughtful and has solid values. We work solely with people who believe in their business and take a huge amount of pride contributing to the community.
