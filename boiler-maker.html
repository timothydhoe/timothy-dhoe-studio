boiler maker

The Brief

Getting sanitary work done is currently a faff, but boiler maker wants to address this by simplyfing the process and helping in promoting family life and the values and benefits of living carefree.

They decided to make life easier and more efficient by digitising the entire sanitary process. From bathroom to lavatory to a quick fix in the sink in your kitchen, all is just a few clicks away. By letting users have a yearly subcription it becomes the most bespoke and flexible model in its business category.

The Opportunity

Do us a favor and look up some logos of the sanitary businesses in your area. We guarantee that almost all of them have either a flame, a droplet and use the colours red and blue. boiler maker is taking the sanitary business to a new level, so their brand and visual language has to reinforce that and stand out amongst many competitors.

The tagline "Everybody Deserves It" answers directly to the philosophy of the brand, working as a motto to maintain the integrity in all their work. This motto is present in everything from the name, the typography, the icons ... Down to every last detail.
